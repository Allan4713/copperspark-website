Our Craftsmanship

Traditional Lost-Wax Casting - 15 Steps to Perfection

1

Design

Our in-house designers and collaborating artists create unique concepts, either independently or based on client requirements.

2

Sculpting

Master sculptors use clay to faithfully reproduce the artistic design, capturing every detail and nuance.

3

Mold Making

Advanced molding technology transforms the sculpture into precise rubber molds for wax pattern reproduction.

4

Wax Injection

Special wax is injected into molds with even distribution, creating accurate wax patterns.

5

Wax Finishing

Skilled craftsmen meticulously refine each wax pattern to ensure flawless surfaces.

6

Spruing & Dipping

Wax patterns are attached with sprues and dipped in ceramic slurry, building multiple layers of refractory material.

7

Drying

Ceramic shells air-dry naturally for 4-7 days to ensure complete curing and structural integrity.

8

Dewaxing

Heating in a specialized furnace melts out the wax, leaving a hollow ceramic shell ready for casting.

9

Pouring

Skilled craftsmen pour molten copper into the preheated ceramic shells at precise temperatures.

10

Knocking Out

After cooling, ceramic shells are carefully broken away to reveal the raw copper casting.

11

Grinding & Polishing

Precision grinding and polishing remove imperfections and create smooth, lustrous surfaces.

12

Color Finishing

Advanced high-temperature coloring techniques apply rich, lasting patinas to enhance each piece's beauty.

13

Quality Inspection

Every piece undergoes rigorous quality control before packaging and shipping.

14

Custom Packaging

Professional packaging with protective materials ensures safe delivery worldwide.

15

Global Shipping

Safe and efficient shipping to customers worldwide via sea and air freight.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us

What is your MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity)?
Our standard MOQ is 50 pieces per design for wholesale orders. For custom/OEM orders, MOQ varies based on design complexity. We welcome small test orders to establish partnership.
What is your production lead time?
Standard orders: 15-25 days after sample approval. Custom/OEM orders: 30-45 days depending on design complexity and order quantity. Rush orders available with additional fees.
Do you accept custom designs?
Absolutely! We specialize in OEM/ODM services. Send us your artwork, sketches, or references, and our design team will create custom molds and produce your unique products.
What are your payment terms?
For new customers: 30% deposit, 70% balance before shipment. For established partners: T/T, L/C, or PayPal accepted. Sample orders: 100% prepayment.
Do you offer samples?
Yes! Sample cost + international shipping applies. Sample fees are refundable with bulk orders over 200 pieces per design.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship worldwide. We work with reliable freight forwarders for competitive sea and air freight rates. FOB Shenzhen/CIF destination available.
Can you provide certificates of quality?
Yes, we provide quality inspection certificates with each shipment. We also support third-party inspection (SGS, Bureau Veritas) upon request.
