## Understanding Copper Figurine Types

### Traditional Chinese Figurines

China has a rich tradition of copper craftsmanship dating back thousands of years. Common traditional figurines include:

- **Buddha Statues**: Symbolizing enlightenment, peace, and spiritual wisdom
- **Guan Yin**: The goddess of compassion and mercy
- **Laughing Buddha (Budai)**: Representing happiness and good fortune
- **Zhong Kui**: The demon queller, offering protection

### Zodiac Animals

Chinese zodiac copper figurines make meaningful gifts and personal decor:

- **Dragon**: Power, strength, and good luck

## Understanding Quality Indicators

### Material Quality

**Pure Copper (T2 Copper)**
- 99.9% copper content
- Rich rose-red color
- Develops beautiful patina over time
- Best for collectors

**Brass (Copper + Zinc)**
- Golden yellow appearance
- More affordable
- Stable color
- Good for everyday decor

**Bronze (Copper + Tin)**
- Warm brown tones
- Traditional appearance
- Excellent for antique-style pieces
- Highly durable

### Craftsmanship Signs

Look for these indicators of quality craftsmanship:

1. **Even patina**: Natural aging should be uniform
2. **Sharp details**: Fine lines and intricate designs should be crisp
3. **Solid weight**: Quality copper figurines have substantial weight
4. **Smooth base**: Should sit flat without wobbling
5. **Secure joints**: Multi-piece figures should connect firmly

### Lost-Wax Casting vs. Other Methods

At CopperSpark Craft, we use the traditional **lost-wax casting** method (失蜡铸造), which offers several advantages:

- **Superior detail**: Captures intricate designs perfectly
- **Hollow interior**: Reduces weight while maintaining strength
- **Unique pieces**: Each casting is slightly unique
- **Traditional craft**: Preserves ancient Chinese metalworking techniques

## FAQ

**Q: How can I tell if a copper figurine is real copper?**
A: Real copper will have a rose-reddish tone and will be attracted to magnets. High-quality pieces will also have substantial weight.

**Q: Will the color change over time?**
